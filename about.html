Meet the Team

We build quietly powerful research tools that respect your work and your privacy — everything runs in your browser, and your material never touches our servers.

Our mission

Serious research, without the gatekeeping

Great research tooling shouldn't require enterprise licences, accounts you don't want, or handing your unpublished work to someone else's server. ItsMyResearch puts a full research workbench — literature discovery, originality signals, methodology design, qualitative analysis, review simulation, and publication-ready formatting — into a plain browser tab: keyless where possible, bring-your-own-AI where useful, and private by architecture rather than by promise.

Principles

How we build

1Private by architecture. Your manuscripts, transcripts, and keys stay in your browser. We can't leak what we never receive.
2Honest about AI. Signals are labelled as signals, uncertainty is shown, and nothing pretends to be proof.
3Transparent to run. Keyless tools stay keyless — no API keys or setup. Runs use clearly priced credits (every tool page shows its cost), and every account starts with a free allowance.
