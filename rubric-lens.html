RubricLens estimates how a grader could read your work against the rubric — it is a revision aid, not the grade. Real graders weigh context, originality, and course norms the model can't see, and an AI reading can miss nuance or misjudge borderline bands. Use it to find weak spots before submission, not to predict your mark.
