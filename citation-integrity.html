Citation Integrity Checker

The check reviewers do first: every in-text citation must appear in the reference list, and every reference must be cited in the text. Upload your paper — get each citation matched, mismatches and orphans flagged, APA style warnings, and a PDF report. Fully in-browser and keyless; your manuscript never leaves this device.
