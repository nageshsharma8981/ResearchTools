Simple, honest pricing

You get a free monthly allowance — 25 for Students, 50 for Educators — and Pro covers heavy use. You always bring your own AI (your key, or the free built-in model) — we never mark up model costs.

How runs are counted

Bigger documents use more credits

A “run” is one tool action — a search, a lookup, a comparison, a model estimation, or an AI generation. Non-AI runs are a flat 1 credit; AI runs scale with how much text you feed them. Every tool page shows its cost next to your balance, so you always know before you click.

Fair-use & the fine print
  • AI model costs are separate and yours: bring your own API key, or use the free built-in browser model (₹0).
  • Every tool run costs credits: standard runs (search, lookup, comparison, model run) are a flat 1 credit; AI generations cost 1–4 by document size. Journal Rankings & Comparison is completely free and unlimited. Browsing, reading, filtering, your Library, and the Journal are always free too.
  • Payments are processed by Razorpay; we never see or store card details. Cancel from your next renewal at any time.
  • The free run requires a confirmed account (one per person — accounts farming free runs get disabled).
